📡 Phone Camera Setup

1. Install IP Webcam (Android) or DroidCam (iOS) on your phone.

2. Connect phone and laptop to the same WiFi.

3. Open the app and tap "Start Server".

4. Note the URL shown (e.g. 192.168.1.5:8080) and use it in the Scan page.
